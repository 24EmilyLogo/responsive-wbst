VENTURING CREW 479

Join Crew 479

Venturing Crew 470 was originally established as Exploring Post 479 in November 1992, and became Venturing Crew 479 in 1999 when Boy Scouts of America(BSA) first established the Venturing program. We recruit and welcome new youth into the crew every year.

What is Venturing?

Venturing is one of the many programs of the BSA and is for people in or entering high school. Venturing breaks up into Crews, these crews do many different things. Anywhere from chilling and playing games to high-adventure. If you can think of a school appropriate activity, there's probably a crew for it.
